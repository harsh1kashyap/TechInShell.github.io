Frequently Asked Questions

I don't have an email account. Can Techinshell help with that?

No problem. Your tech can help select an email provider and then get your account set up.

I'm having trouble with my email. Can Techinshell help?

Yes, Techinshell can help if you're having trouble with your email. We troubleshoot common email-related problems. This might include issues such as trouble with sending or receiving messages, missing or duplicated emails and undeliverable emails.

I'm having trouble with my Google account, but not specifically my email.

We can help with that. Go ahead and book your service, detailing the issue you're experiencing.

What is included in an Email or Online Account Setup with TechInShell ?

This Email or Online Account Setup service with Hello Tech includes: • Troubleshooting or setting up one active email account • Configuring account settings and features • Demonstrating webmail functionality • Instant online support through HelloTech NOW should you choose to use it instead of in-home service

How much does TechInShell's Email or Online Account Setup service cost?

HelloTech's Email or Online Account Setup service has a base price of $29 and starts at $0 for members of Smart Home Plus. While setting up a new account does not result in any additional charges, if your service requires troubleshooting an existing account, an extra charge of $50 will apply. If you opt for in-home service instead of Techinshell NOW, an additional $50 charge applies.

I'm having trouble with another online account. Can my tech help with that?

Yes. When you book your service, you can detail the issue you're experiencing. Our techs can assist you with troubleshooting problems you're having with an online account, even if it's not an email account specifically.
